Careers
A culture of innovation

Versett is designed to be a place for doing meaningful work. Our team is tight-knit and dedicated to building great products. Send us something you’ve made that you’re proud of and we’ll be in touch.

Opening Roles

At Versett, a Product Designer is responsible for how products look, feel and move. You will be involved at all stages of the process and assist in the concept, design, prototype, and test products to ensure that everything we build is of the highest quality. This role spans many design disciplines: user research, information architecture, prototyping, ideation, interaction design and visual design. Sometimes you will be on the front-lines, working on brand-new problems, figuring out what to build. Other times, we’re deep in the details of a project and figuring out how to build it.

Product Designers at Versett have the following:

  • You have experience building and shipping high-impact digital products for consumer or commercial audiences and have the portfolio to prove it.
  • You love exploring the latest and greatest in design tools while keeping emerging design patterns and visual treatments on your radar.
  • You will maintain, guide, and push the boundaries of design systems and patterns.
  • You can clearly communicate concepts and design rationale through sketches, wireframes, high fidelity comps, prototypes and presentations.
  • You are a strong conceptual thinker with a demonstrated ability to understand customer needs, motivations and behaviors, and know how to translate this understanding into the project design.
  • You will collaborate well and are a team player.
